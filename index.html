A METAVERSE WORLD

The Term "METAVERSE" was coined by Author Neal Stephenson in his 1992 novel "Snow Crash".

A place of self actualization

Stephenson's book provided no specific defination of the Metaverse, but what he described was a persisteen virtual world that reached, interacted with, and affected nearly every part of human existence. It was a place for labor and leisure, for self-actualization as well as physical exhaustion.
